Anagram Solver dictionaries — provenance
========================================

These word lists back The Codex's Anagram Solver. Each was reduced to lowercase
a-z, one word per line, with accents folded onto their base letter (á→a, ç→c,
ã→a) and one-letter entries dropped. They are fetched lazily at runtime.

en.txt — English (~172k words)
  Source: ENABLE word list (public domain), via dolph/dictionary (enable1.txt).

pt.txt — Portuguese (~315k words)
  Source: pythonprobr/palavras (palavras.txt).

la.txt — Latin (~265k inflected forms)
  Source: form column of bbloomf/verbalatina (latin-analyses.txt), derived from
  Whitaker's Words / Lewis & Short.
